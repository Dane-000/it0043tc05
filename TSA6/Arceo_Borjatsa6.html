What do we know about Inside Out 2?

Inside Out 2, in particular, had broad appeal across age demographics. Riley, the hero of the first film, turns into a teen and copes with emotions like anxiety and embarrassment. The movie speaks to some rather grown-up themes and no doubt attracted moviegoers beyond their teen years themselves.

How old is Riley in Inside Out 2??

This past Friday, Pixar released a sequel to Inside Out. In part two, Riley is now thirteen years old and she's pushed into a new development stage, which brings to life new emotions inside of her: Anxiety, Ennui, Nostalgia, and Embarrassment, each one a complex character in its own right. She is changing.
